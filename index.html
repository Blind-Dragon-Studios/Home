Our Manifesto

We don't make jump scares.
We craft dread.

In the Karst caves of Slovenia, ancient things sleep in the darkness. We've spent years studying their whispers, translating their nightmares into interactive experiences that linger long after the screen goes dark.

We believe in atmosphere over action, in the terror of anticipation, in the horror of what you don't see. We are Triple-I: Independent, Immersive, Intense.
