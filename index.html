How we build

Atmosphere first. Always.

We’re a small, focused team. That means fewer distractions and more care: tighter sound, stronger visual storytelling, and design choices that serve the feeling—not the checklist.

You’ll see it in our pacing and our worlds: we prefer eerie details over cheap surprises. If we do a jump scare, it earns its place. Most of the time, we’d rather let the environment do the work.
