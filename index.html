Featured project

Island Mystery

A sun-drenched Caribbean island. A string of impossible crimes. You arrive as an outsider tasked with uncovering the truth—but every local has secrets, every paradise hides something rotten, and the island itself seems to want you gone.

Inspired by the charm and intrigue of Death in Paradise, Island Mystery blends investigation gameplay with atmospheric exploration, rich characters, and a storyline that twists when you least expect it.

How we build

Atmosphere first. Always.

We're a small, focused team. That means fewer distractions and more care: tighter sound, stronger visual storytelling, and design choices that serve the feeling—not the checklist.

You'll see it in our pacing and our worlds: we prefer eerie details over cheap surprises. Whether it's a tropical paradise hiding something sinister or a quiet room that doesn't feel right—the environment does the work.

What we stand for

Games that feel intentional.

A few pillars we hold ourselves to—so every project feels cohesive and memorable.

01

Compelling mysteries

We craft layered puzzles and narratives that reward attention, curiosity, and careful observation.

02

Worlds with a story

03

Audio you feel

Sound design is half the experience. We aim for grounded, immersive audio that pulls you deeper in.
