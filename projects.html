Island Mystery

A sun-drenched Caribbean island. A string of impossible crimes. You arrive as an outsider tasked with uncovering the truth—but every local has secrets, every paradise hides something rotten, and the island itself seems to want you gone.

Inspired by the charm and intrigue of Death in Paradise, Island Mystery blends investigation gameplay with atmospheric exploration, rich characters, and a storyline that twists when you least expect it.

Mystery Investigation Story-driven Single-player Atmospheric

About the game

What makes Island Mystery different

01

Investigation-first gameplay

Examine crime scenes, interview suspects, piece together evidence, and build your case. The answers are there—if you look closely enough.

02

A living island

Explore a vibrant Caribbean setting with its own culture, routines, and hidden corners. The locals have lives—and lies—of their own.

03

Twists you won't see coming

Layered narratives that reward paying attention. Just when you think you've solved it, the island reveals another layer.

Development roadmap

Where we are

We're building Island Mystery step by step, sharing progress with our community along the way.

Q1 2026

Concept & Pre-production

World design, narrative framework, character concepting, and core mechanic prototyping. Building the tone and feel of the island.

Q2 2026

Vertical slice

A playable slice demonstrating investigation flow, NPC interactions, and the island atmosphere. First internal playtests.

Q3 2026

Community playtests

Inviting Discord Vanguard members to test early builds, gather feedback, and help shape the experience before broader release.

2027

Full production & launch prep

Content completion, polish passes, marketing, and preparing for launch. More details shared as we get closer.
