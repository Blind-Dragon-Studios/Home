About the Studio

The Vision

Who We Are

Blind Dragon Studios is a Triple-I (Independent, Immersive, Intense) game studio specializing in atmospheric horror experiences. We believe that true horror doesn't rely on jump scares—it builds through atmosphere, anticipation, and the fear of what you cannot see.

Our team consists of passionate creators who share a common vision: to craft games that linger in your mind long after you've stopped playing.

Our Values

Atmosphere Over Action

We craft worlds where tension builds naturally through environmental storytelling and audio design.

Player Experience First

Every decision is made with the player's emotional journey in mind.

Quality Over Quantity

We'd rather release one exceptional experience than ten forgettable ones.

Community Driven

Our Vanguard community shapes development from the earliest stages.
