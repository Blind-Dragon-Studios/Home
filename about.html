About the studio

Built for atmosphere.

Who we are

Blind Dragon Studios is an independent team building atmospheric games that immerse you in rich, story-driven worlds. Our goal is simple: create tension you can feel, mysteries worth solving, and worlds you want to explore even when something feels off.

We started with a love for narrative-driven experiences—from tense horror to sun-soaked mystery. Our debut project, Island Mystery, draws on the charm and intrigue of shows like Death in Paradise to deliver something fresh in the investigation genre.

What we value

A few principles we use to make decisions—whether it's a feature, a scene, or the final mix.

Atmosphere over noise

We build tension and intrigue through space, lighting, and sound—not constant action.

Player experience first

Every choice is measured against what the player feels in the moment.

Care > content

We'd rather ship something memorable than something rushed.

Community feedback

Our Discord helps us iterate early—when changes still matter most.
