About the studio

Built for dread.

Who we are

Blind Dragon Studios is an independent team building atmospheric horror games. Our goal is simple: create tension you can feel, and worlds you want to explore even when you’re not sure you should.

We don’t chase constant jump scares. We care about pacing, mood, and the uncomfortable question of what’s just out of sight.

What we value

A few principles we use to make decisions—whether it’s a feature, a scene, or the final mix.

Atmosphere over noise

We build tension through space, lighting, and sound—not constant action.

Player experience first

Every choice is measured against what the player feels in the moment.

Care > content

We’d rather ship something memorable than something rushed.

Community feedback

Our Discord helps us iterate early—when changes still matter.
