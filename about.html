The Studio

Our Origin

Founded in 2026 in the heart of Slovenia

Blind Dragon Studios operates virtually, digging deep into the Karst folklore that has shaped our homeland for millennia. Our team is scattered across Europe, but our roots remain firmly planted in the limestone caves of the Slovenian Karst.

We prioritize atmosphere over action. We are high-fidelity. We are Triple-I: Independent, Immersive, Intense.

In the darkness, we found clarity. In the silence, we heard stories that had waited centuries to be told. We didn't choose horror— horror chose us.

— Founding Manifesto, 2026
